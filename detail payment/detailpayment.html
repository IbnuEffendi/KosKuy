Payment Instructions

Please follow these steps to complete your payment:

  1. Enter your full name as it appears on your bank account.
  2. Use the transaction reference number provided in the confirmation email.
  3. Select the date of your transaction.
  4. Ensure the amount matches the total shown on your invoice.
  5. Select your payment method. We accept bank transfers and credit cards.
